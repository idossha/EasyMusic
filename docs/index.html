Features

User-Friendly GUI

No command line required. Simple, intuitive interface for everyone.

High-Quality Audio

Downloads music in 320kbps MP3 format for the best listening experience.

Multiple Formats

Download individual tracks, full albums, or entire playlists with ease.

Real-Time Progress

Live download tracking with detailed progress bars and console output.

Cross-Platform

Works on macOS (Apple Silicon) and Linux. Download binaries for your platform.

Spotify & YouTube

Download from both Spotify and YouTube with a simple toggle.

How to Use

1

Paste a Music URL

Copy the Spotify or YouTube URL of the track, album, or playlist you want to download.

2

Toggle Source (Optional)

Use the toggle button to switch between Spotify and YouTube mode if needed.

3

Select Output Folder

Choose where you want to save your downloaded music files.

4

Click Download

Hit the download button and watch the progress in real-time.
